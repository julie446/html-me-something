Chai Tea

Chai tea is great for relaxing on a chilly day. I make it with ginger as opposed to cardamon.

  • Red Label Tea (available at Namaste India at 103rd & Metcalf)
  • milk (2% or skim)
  • ginger
  • sugar

Remove the peel from the ginger and cut it into thin slices. Boil a little water and the ginger in a pan over medium heat. Add 2 teaspoons of Red Label Tea. Add one cup of milk. Bring back to boil. Strain the tea. Add one teaspoon sugar.

Momos

Momos can be made with any type of filling. Ground beef is best, but I don't cook meat.

  • flour
  • ingredients for filling
    • tofu
    • vermicelli rice noodles
    • chives
    • ground black pepper
  • Sriracha sauce (aka Rooster sauce) for dipping

Combine flour and water to dough consistency. Knead. Roll into very small and thin pancakes. Chop and combine filling. This video explains how to seal the momos (starting at 3 minutes into the video). Then steam the momos until done.
